# **A Study: Statcreep in Fire Emblem Heroes**

Fire Emblem Heroes has been out for nearly 10 years now. During this time, over 1300 units have been added to the game's ever-growing roster. Each year, we see an increase in base stat totals and ceiling breaks for certain stats. It's only inevitable that the game sees stat increases, since it incentivizes players to spend currency - in-game or real - to pull for the latest units.

This brings up a few questions: **how significant are the changes in stats since the game's launch? What changes can we expect going into the future?**

In order to explore this question, I will conduct an analysis of the different stats of units over the years, explore to what degree statlines have changed across versions, and make a few predicitons for statlines going into the future.
